Game Rules

Basic Rules

  • Players place their symbols (X, O, N) in turns.
  • Winning patterns:
    • 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, diagonal)
    • 2x2 square of the same symbol
  • If no one wins and all cells are filled, the game ends in a draw.

Series Rules

  • Play a series of 1, 3, 5, or 10 games.
  • Player X vs two AI opponents: Blocker (O) and Hunter (N).
  • Each win earns 1 point; most points win the series.
  • A tied series is declared a draw.
