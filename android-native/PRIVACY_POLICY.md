# Privacy Policy for Tic Tac Toe Trio

**Last updated: May 15, 2026**

## Overview

Tic Tac Toe Trio ("the App") is a single-player mobile game developed by Iniyan. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All game data (scores, achievements, settings, and progress) is stored locally on your device using the Android Room database and SharedPreferences.

## Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device:
- Player name (entered by you)
- Game statistics (wins, losses, draws, streaks)
- Achievement unlock status
- XP and level progress
- Skin and customization preferences
- Daily challenge completion status
- Game history and move records

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Internet Access

The App does not require internet access and functions entirely offline.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no ads, no in-app purchases, and no social features.

## Data Deletion

All locally stored data can be deleted by clearing the App's data through your device settings (Settings → Apps → Tic Tac Toe Trio → Clear Data) or by uninstalling the App.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
