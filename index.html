Game Rules

Basic Rules

  • The game is played on a 5x5 grid.
  • Players take turns placing their symbol (X, O, or N) in empty cells.
  • The goal is to create one of the following winning patterns:
    • 4 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally)
    • A 2x2 square of the same symbol
  • The first player to create a winning pattern wins the game.
  • If all cells are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw.

Series Rules

  • Players can choose to play a series of games: 1, 3, 5, or 10 games.
  • The player (X) competes against two AI opponents: Blocker (O) and Hunter (N).
  • Each game in the series is played independently.
  • The winner of each game earns a point.
  • At the end of the series, the player with the most points wins the series.
  • In case of a tie, the series is declared a draw.
